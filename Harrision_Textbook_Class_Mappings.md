# Harrison’s Principles of Internal Medicine Class Mappings

This document summarizes the taxonomy mapping for two ECG datasets: **SPH Dataset (Harrison’s-based)** and **CINC-2020 Dataset**.

---

## SPH Dataset: Harrison’s-based Taxonomy Mapping

- **A:** Normal EKG
- **B:**
  - Junctional premature complexes
  - Junctional escape complexes
  - Junctional tachycardia
  - Sinus tachycardia
  - Sinus bradycardia
  - Sinus arrhythmia
- **C:**
  - Atrial fibrillation (including with rapid or slow ventricular response)
  - Atrial flutter
  - Atrial premature complexes (occasional, frequent, couplets, bigeminy, aberrancy)
  - Atrial premature complexes, nonconducted
- **D:**
  - Ventricular premature complexes (occasional, frequent, couplets, bigeminy, polymorphic)
  - Ventricular preexcitation
- **E:**
  - AV conduction ratio
  - Prolonged PR interval
  - Second-degree AV block (Mobitz I and Mobitz II)
  - 2:1 AV block
  - Varying AV conduction
  - Advanced (high-grade) AV block
  - Complete (third-degree) AV block
- **F:**
  - Left anterior fascicular block
  - Left posterior fascicular block
  - Left bundle-branch block
  - Incomplete right bundle-branch block
  - Right bundle-branch block
- **G:** Right-axis deviation; Left-axis deviation
- **H:** Low voltage
- **I:** Left atrial enlargement
- **J:** Left ventricular hypertrophy; Right ventricular hypertrophy
- **K:**
  - ST segment abnormality
  - ST–T change due to ventricular hypertrophy
  - ST deviation with T-wave change
  - Early repolarization
- **L:** Anterior, Inferior, Anteroseptal, and Extensive anterior myocardial infarction (Acute, Recent, Old)
- **M:** Prolonged QT interval

---

## CINC-2020 Dataset

- **A:** Sinus rhythm
- **B:**
  - Paced rhythm
  - Sinus tachycardia
  - Sinus bradycardia
  - Sinus arrhythmia
- **C:**
  - Atrial fibrillation
  - Atrial flutter
  - Atrial premature complexes (premature atrial contractions)
  - Supraventricular premature beats
- **D:** Ventricular premature beats
- **E:** Prolonged PR interval; Atrioventricular block
- **F:**
  - Left anterior fascicular block
  - Left bundle-branch block
  - Incomplete right bundle-branch block
  - Right bundle-branch block
  - Nonspecific intraventricular conduction disorder
- **G:** Right-axis deviation; Left-axis deviation
- **H:** Low QRS voltage
- **I:** T-wave abnormality; T-wave inversion
- **J:** Prolonged QT interval
